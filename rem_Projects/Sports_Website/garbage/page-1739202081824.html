Detailed Game Information

Kabaddi Overview

🕒

Age

4000+ years of history

🏃

Players

7 players per team

🌍

Popularity

International recognition in Asian Games

Benefits

  • Builds stamina and reflexes
  • Enhances agility and coordination
  • Promotes teamwork and strategy

How to Play

Team Formation

Two teams of seven players each face off on the court

Raiding

Raider enters opponent's half while chanting "Kabaddi-Kabaddi"

Scoring

Points awarded for successful raids and defensive stops

Winning

Team with the most points at the end of the match wins

Rich Heritage of Indian Sports

Discover the ancient origins and cultural significance of traditional Indian games

Ancient Origins

📜

Vedic Period

Many games originated during the Vedic period, serving as both recreational activities and warrior training methods.

⚔️

Warrior Training

Traditional games were integral to developing physical prowess and strategic thinking in ancient warriors.

🏺

Cultural Heritage

These games have been passed down through generations, preserving India's rich sporting heritage.

Evolution Through Time

Ancient Era (4000+ Years Ago)

Games like Kabaddi originated and were played in their earliest forms

Medieval Period

Games evolved and became part of military training

Modern Era

Traditional games adapted to modern formats and gained international recognition
